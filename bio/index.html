Founder proof

QChat is my taste test.

QChat is a consumer AI product for delegating everyday tasks by voice or text: food, groceries, workouts, housing, and other city workflows. The important idea is simple: the agent should produce outcomes, not just answers.

AI-native UX voice delegation consumer agents

City errands through agent workflows.

I designed and built the product around a daily delegation model: users say what they need, QChat routes the task to the right agent, and the agent keeps context across the workflow.

  • Food and grocery ordering flows with carts, totals, and confirmation moments.
  • Fitness and nutrition flows that turn natural language into structured records.
  • Real-estate search workflows designed around agency partnerships and user intent.
